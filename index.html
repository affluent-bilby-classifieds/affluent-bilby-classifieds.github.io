Welcome to Affluent Bilby Classifieds. We are an open-source business looking to become a non-for-profit foundation. Our code is written in Python in collaboration with the open source community. Our goal is to produce a print media publication using automated technology that will enable all people to access classified and employment ads even if they don't have access to a computer. We are built from the ground up to be community owned and operated.
